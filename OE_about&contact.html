... ABOUT

Open Eyes (OE) was created by our founder, Emmanuel Bonsu, as a means of remedying a common yet uncommon problem. Individuals interested in partaking in activities requiring cameras and/or camera equipment may go researching the proper equipment to purchase, yet may get misled due to a oversaturation of camera information on the web. Open Eyes' website is to serve as a one-stop-shop, providing basic need-to-know knowledge about cameras while also providing a way of pairing an individual or a cause with a camera. Open Eyes is designed for any photo/film consumer, regardless of expertise level.
